About Me

I’ve worked my way from part-time associate to store manager in several wonderful retail companies, wearing many hats along the way. I’ve enjoyed and excelled in many areas of retail business, including visual merchandising, digital and print media, inventory and point-of-sale management, and eCommerce, all to support a thriving business while delivering an exceptional customer experience.
My most recent endeavour allowed me to focus on the “back-end” systems of a successful “click-and-mortar” small business (one with both physical and web-based stores). I led successful marketing campaigns, both in print and online advertisements, and created systems to streamline the many types of product orders needed to keep the stores well stocked. Creating solutions in these companies began sparking my interest in exploring programming & design and solving problems in the world of web development.
As a full stack web developer, I am passionate about implementing cutting edge technologies to heighten the user experience and being an effective member of an efficient team.
